About Me

As a ranch worker, most of my life has been spent on a small ranch in central Texas, doing farm work and helping care for livestock. I've been blessed with excellent elders to learn from, and have a love for working with others from it.

I was born and raised a Christian, and stand strong as one today. I give all praise and glory to the Lord and don't believe I can accomplish anything without Him.

I was given an interest in science and computers by my grandfather when I was fairly young, though I didn't actually start programming until early 2020 when I started with Codecademy. I soon got involved working in their community, and mid 2020 I joined their Discord. I got my first experience working with a group on a programming project, helping to build the initial version of the Codecademy Community Moderation Bot. I was promoted to moderator on the CC Discord server in 2021, and gained a lot of great experiences working with and helping other programmers.

In early 2022 Skillsoft bought Codecademy and I felt it was time for me to move on from it. I resigned as a moderator in April, and joined a group of other volunteers to begin building the Project Nu forums.

My fascination with technology is balanced with a love for a more "primitive" day. I love the outdoors and traditional trades. With ranch life you pick up many odd skills and so I have some knowledge of woodworking, plumbing, fencing, and electrical. I've fiddled with hunting, and taxidermy as a trade. For physical fitness I love martial arts, and train often in brazillion jiujitsu.
